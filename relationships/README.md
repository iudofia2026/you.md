# Relationships

Create profiles for people in your life.

## Directories
- **family/** - Family members
- **friends/** - Friends
- **mentors/** - Professional mentors
- **connections/** - Professional connections

## Profile Template

```markdown
# [Name]

## Relationship
- **Relation**: [How you know them]
- **Pronouns**: [they/them, she/her, he/him]

## Background
- **Occupation**: [What they do]
- **Location**: [Where they live]

## Relationship Dynamics
- **Communication style**: [How you interact]
- **Shared interests**: [What you bond over]

## Notes for AI Agents
When helping me communicate with [Name]:
- [Style notes]
- [Topics to ask about]
```
